Know Innocent's Family and Hobbies


Family life



Kamvelihle Innocent is the son of Mongezi Mandisi Jika and Fikisiwe Khethiwe Jika, but got a different surname because he was born before marriage. It is customary in Xhosa community that a child should use mother's surname if born before marriage or else the father will have to pay a couple of cows, unfortunately his father could afford the price.

Hobbies



Kamvelihle usually spent most of his spare time on sports mostly soccer and rugby, some of his time is spent so much playing computer games and board games. He also listen to music a lot when he is not in public and/or doing it things that do not need so much concetration such as home chores. Some of his time is spent on computer programming and reading novels and short stories.
