Who is Innocent?



Kamvelihle Innocent started schooling at the age of 6 years, when he first attended his grade 1 class at Mjanyelwa Junior Secondary School in Bizana in the rural areas of Eastern Cape, South Africa. He went on attending at Mjanyelwa Junior Secondary School until grade 9 in year 2010, and then, he started his high school eduacation at Bizana Senior Secondary School in the year 2011, and then matriculated from there in the year 2013.

Innocent persuaded his dream in Information Technology by attending at Walter Sisulu University at Buffalo City Campus (Postdam Site), in the year 2015 after a gap year. He has been one of the bright students in the department of IT in the campus with some few departmental awards (Best Student in Technical Programming I and Best Student in Overall Software Development II) and one national award (South Africa's No. 3 in Standard Bank Technology Impact Challenge 2017 in Idea Chain Challenge) through his way obtaining National Diploma (Cum Laude).

Kamvelihle is currently registered as an IT B-Tech student, specializing in Software Development in Walter Sisulu University of Technology and Science, got one award in hand already (as South Africa's No. 1 in Standard Bank Technology Impact Challenge in Idea Chain Challenge) and half-way through his dissertation which is necessarily for B-Tech students.
